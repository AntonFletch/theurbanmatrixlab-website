About The Urban Matrix Lab LLC

The Urban Matrix Lab LLC is a technology-supported property solutions company focused on improving buildings, environments, and communities through intelligent maintenance, rehabilitation, inspection services, and operational systems.

Our mission is to combine practical craftsmanship with modern technology to create healthier, safer, and more efficient living and working environments throughout Western Montana.

Our Services

Property Maintenance

Routine maintenance, repairs, property inspections, turnovers, and operational support for residential and commercial properties.

Property Rehabilitation

Building improvements, restoration coordination, cleanup, repairs, and modernization projects.

Indoor Environment Solutions

Indoor air quality assessments, environmental improvement planning, moisture awareness, and healthier building strategies.

Inspection Services

Property condition assessments, documentation, maintenance evaluations, and improvement recommendations.

Smart Property Operations

Technology-supported systems designed to improve efficiency, communication, tracking, and long-term property performance.

Commercial Support Services

Recurring maintenance solutions for property managers, businesses, housing communities, and organizations.

The UMX Vision

The Urban Matrix Lab LLC is building a future-focused property ecosystem where intelligent systems, sustainable practices, and skilled operations work together.

Our long-term vision includes advanced property management technology, smart environments, sustainable housing concepts, and scalable service networks designed to strengthen communities.
